I'm finally biting the bullet, and trying to make myself a website. The reasons for this are multiple:

1. I've got a few projects in the works that are nearing publication stage, and I'd like to have somewhere to talk about them, advertise them, and otherwise share (e.g., reprints).
1. It's good to have a public presence, especially as a scientist.
1. I've been learning more and more coding since my first MATLAB class in the Northern Hemisphere autumn of 2016, as I now dabble in Python and bash for my work. A website that can host blog posts is useful to me as a personal record of fixes, tricks, tips, and solutions to such coding work. Hopefully, I'll limit the number of times my browser gets cluttered with tabs while I search for that *one* StackOverflow solution I found previously. Maybe I'll even learn a little git in the process.

So, welcome to my tottering, infant-like efforts of a webpage!
